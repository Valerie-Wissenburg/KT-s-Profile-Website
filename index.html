Bio

Ya Boi KT

Born and raised in Milwaukee, WI, Ya Boi KT has always possessed a love for music. From singing solos in his local church to winning the Director's Award in his grade school band, this young artist has many proud accomplishments under his belt. While attending Rufus King IB High School, he also was a part of the now 8-time city championship drumline.
